# Niklas Luhmann

20th-century German sociologist, one of the most prolific social theorists in history with 70+ books and 400+ papers. His secret weapon was a Zettelkasten (slip-box) containing approximately 90,000 cards.

## Relevance to LLM Wiki

Extended Brain uses Luhmann as the primary counterpoint to Karpathy's LLM Wiki. The critical distinction: for Luhmann, the act of writing cards in one's own words *was* the thinking itself. The friction of translation — when language breaks down and reveals gaps in understanding — is not inefficiency to be eliminated, but the mechanism of cognitive integration.
