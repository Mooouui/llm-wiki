# NotebookLM

Google's AI-powered notebook product. Supports uploading sources, asking questions, generating podcasts, video overviews, mind maps, and flashcards.

## Key Contrast with LLM Wiki

The article describes NotebookLM as a "well-equipped library reading room" — efficient while you're there, but reset for the next visitor. Sources are static by default, no autonomous auditing, no cross-corpus suggestions. Data lives on Google's cloud under Google's terms. LLM Wiki is described as a "garden you tend year after year" — persistent, compounding, self-maintaining, with full data sovereignty.

| Dimension | NotebookLM | LLM Wiki |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Knowledge persistence | Per-session | Permanent, compounding |
| Cross-referencing | Manual | Automatic, maintained |
| Data ownership | Google cloud | Local plain text |
| Maintenance | User-driven | LLM-driven |
