# Obsidian

Markdown-based knowledge management application that serves as the "frontend IDE" for browsing and interacting with the LLM Wiki. Key features in the LLM Wiki context:

- **Web Clipper**: Browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) for one-click web-to-Markdown capture into raw/
- **Graph View**: Visualizes wiki structure — hub pages, orphans, and clusters at a glance
- **Dataview plugin**: Queries page frontmatter to generate dynamic tables
- **Marp plugin**: Generates slides from wiki content
- **Local images**: Configurable attachment path to raw/assets/ for offline image storage
