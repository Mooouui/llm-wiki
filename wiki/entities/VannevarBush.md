# Vannevar Bush

American engineer and inventor who in 1945 published "As We May Think" in The Atlantic Monthly, describing a hypothetical device called the Memex. His core insight: human minds work by association, not alphabetical indexing.

## Relevance to LLM Wiki

Karpathy cites Bush's Memex as a direct ancestor of LLM Wiki. The Memex concept directly inspired Douglas Engelbart (mouse, personal computing), Ted Nelson (hypertext, 1965), and Tim Berners-Lee (World Wide Web, 1989). The missing puzzle piece Bush couldn't solve: who does the maintenance? LLMs answer that question.
