# Service Graph Connectors

Service Graph Connectors are pre-built integrations that connect [[ServiceNow]] with external systems to import, normalize, and maintain high-quality data in the [[CMDB]]. They provide the fastest time-to-value for gaining visibility across the operational estate.

## Connector Categories

- Partner cloud providers (AWS, Azure)
- Endpoint/IT asset management (SCCM, Intune, Jamf)
- Monitoring and observability tools
- Security solutions
- Software, server, or network applications
- Operational Technology (OT) applications
- Partner-built connectors

## Key Features

- **IRE-native** — all connectors route data through the [[IRE]] to prevent duplicates and maintain data integrity
- **CSDM-conformant** — each connector is reviewed for [[CSDM]] compliance
- **Built on IntegrationHub ETL** — leverages [[IntegrationHubETL]] transform maps, allowing administrators to preview and adjust mappings
- ITOM Visibility licensing required (except for SCCM)
